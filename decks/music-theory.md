### Card
Q: What are the notes in a **C major scale**?

A:
C – D – E – F – G – A – B – C

Pattern: **W W H W W W H**
(W = whole step, H = half step)

---

### Card
Q: What is the **circle of fifths**?

A:
An arrangement of all 12 keys in ascending perfect-fifth order:

**C → G → D → A → E → B → F♯/G♭ → D♭ → A♭ → E♭ → B♭ → F → C**

Moving clockwise adds one sharp; counter-clockwise adds one flat.

### Card
Q: How do you build a **dominant 7th chord**?

A:
Root + major 3rd + perfect 5th + minor 7th.

In C: **C – E – G – B♭**

Formula: 1 – 3 – 5 – ♭7
